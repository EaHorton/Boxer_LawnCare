Weed Control & Fertilization

At Boxer Lawn & Landscape, we understand the importance of maintaining a healthy and beautiful lawn. Our licensed professional provides comprehensive weed control and fertilization services tailored to meet the unique needs of your landscape.

Weed Control

Our licensed technician uses safe and effective methods to target and eliminate unwanted weeds, ensuring they don’t compete with your lawn’s grass for essential nutrients. We carefully select treatments that are appropriate for your specific lawn type, applying them at the right time to maximize results while protecting your plants, pets, and the environment.

Fertilization

To keep your lawn vibrant and lush, we apply high-quality fertilizers that deliver the right balance of nutrients at the right time of year. Our professionals are trained to assess your lawn’s individual needs, providing custom fertilization schedules to enhance growth, improve root development, and give you a green, thriving lawn.

By relying on a licensed professional, you can rest assured that your lawn is in good hands. We ensure the proper application of products and techniques to provide the best care for your outdoor space. Let us take care of your lawn so you can enjoy a beautiful, weed-free yard all year round!
